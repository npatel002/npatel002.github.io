Making Chocolate Chip Cookies

Everyone loves chocolate chip cookies, so I decided to share the recipe that I use.
You will need the following things:

Equipment

  • Measuring Spoons
  • Measuring Cups
  • KitchenAid Mixer
  • Bowls
  • Spatula
  • Parchment paper

Ingredients

Soft Butter 1 cup
Brown Sugar 1 cup
White Sugar 1 cup
Flour 3 cup
Sea Salt 1 tsp
Baking Powder 1/2 tsp
Baking Soda 1 tsp
Chocolate Chips 2 cup
Eggs 2 large
Vanilla Extract 2 tsp

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375o F. Line a baking pan with parchment paper and set aside.
  2. In a bowl, mix all the dry ingredients together.
  3. In a separate bowl, cream butter and sugar until combined.
  4. Beat in eggs and vanilla extract until fluffy.
  5. Slowly, add and mix the dry ingredients until combied.
  6. Add in the chocolate chips and mix well.
  7. Make small round balls or use a small cookie scoop and place the dough on the pan that we prepared earlier.
  8. Bake in the preheated over for approximately 8-10 minutes.
